Hair Extensions

I am qualified in a range of hair extensions. These include hot bonds, naked weave, Rapunzel Weave, Racoon Luxe links, micro rings, nano rings, tapes and pre bonded. To find out the best type for you and your hair book in for a free consultation.

Permanent Straightening

This is a wonderful treatment that will make your hair permanently straight. Keeping it silky soft and frizz free. This treatment lasts until your hair grows out and will need to be topped up yearly. Book in for a free consultation to see if this is the right treatment for you.

Hairloss Solutions

To help you feel like you again I offer solutions including the Mesh Integration System, Wig fittings and personalisation for when your recovering post hair loss. Whether it's due to medical or physical, Hair in recovery is an amazing service to give back longer hair whilst your natural hair grows through.
